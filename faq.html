Frequently asked questions:

  • How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

    • Save the document you want to print in .pdf format. Then open the document
    and click on the print icon. Walk to one of the printers and use your student
    pass to print the document.

  • How can you scan a document a send it to your laptop at HZ?

    • Log in with your student pass at one of the printers. Afterwards select Scanning.
    Place the document on the feeder or glass plate. Go through the following steps:
    Scan and Send -> Scan to me -> Yes. Press the Start button. Once the document is
    scanned, you are able to send it over by pressing Start Sending.

  • What do you need to do when you are sick / show symptoms of coronavirus?

    • If you show symptoms or if you think you have coronavirus, immideatly inform
    the studycoordinator and call in sick. If the symptoms stay infrom your gp, or the ggd.

  • How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

    • You can book a project space by going to this link:
    https://topdesk.hz.nl/tas/public/ssp/content/page/locationreservationplanner

  • What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

    • If you have your student pass, go to the reception and they will prepare the card.
    Once done at the reception the next time you drive to school you can use the pass
    to enter the parking lot. Here there are dedicated parking spots for the HZ.
