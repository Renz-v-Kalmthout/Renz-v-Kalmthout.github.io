Theory:

Definition:

Motivation is the driving force of a person, the type of force is dependend on the situation. A person may study harder just to get a better job in the future. Another may try to save someone's life. The reasoning behind these actions is the motive. And in above said examples these are completely different. According to the article on the website: "https://www.studyandexam.com/motivation-meaning-types.html" There are two common forms of Motivation:

  • Intinsic motivation
  • Extrinsic motivation

    • Intrinsic motivation:

    This type of motivation is mostly based on internal wants and needs. E.g.: "I am hungry, I am going to eat something." But it is not limited to being curious, or needed to be loved.

    Extrinsic motivation:

    This type of motivation is based on external wants and needs. For example: "Studying harder for a test, to get a better grade." This does not mean it doesn't have an effect on the internal feeling of an individual.
