My motivation:

Theory:

Definition:

Textbook definition according to the Cambridge Dictionary:

"The willingness to do something, or something that causes this willingness."

Motivation is the driving force of a person, the type of force is dependend on the situation. A person may study harder just to get a better job in the future. Another may try to save someone's life. The reasoning behind these actions is the motive. And in above said examples these are completely different. According to the article on the website: "https://www.studyandexam.com/motivation-meaning-types.html" There are two common forms of Motivation:

  • Intinsic motivation
  • Extrinsic motivation

    • However looking at more examples shows that "http://changingminds.org/explanations/motivation/four_motivations.htm" Gives us two more variants on motivations that are also very applicable to current day:

  • Introjected motivation
  • Identified motivation

    • Intrinsic motivation:

    This type of motivation is mostly based on internal wants and needs. E.g.: "I am hungry, I am going to eat something." But it is not limited to being curious, or needed to be loved.

    Extrinsic motivation:

    This type of motivation is based on external wants and needs. For example: "Studying harder for a test, to get a better grade." This does not mean it doesn't have an effect on the internal feeling of an individual.

    Introjected motivation:

    It feels simmular to Intrinsic motivation, however if not done the person will feel guilty.

    Identified motivation:

    This means that the person knows he needs to do something, but hasn't done anything about it yet.

    Preformance motivation:

    Je bent in redelijke mate intrinsiek gemotiveerd en maar weinig extrinsiek gemotiveerd. Dat je redelijk intrinsiek gemotiveerd bent betekent dat je het best leuk vindt om te studeren. Dat je weinig extrinsiek gemotiveerd bent wil zeggen dat het je weinig kan schelen wat je met je studie kunt bereiken: een diploma, geld, status of de erkenning van de mensen waar je van houdt. Je interesses zijn voor jou belangrijker.

    In zekere maten ben ik het eens met wat hierboven wordt gezegd, daarentegen ben ik wel doelgericht. Niet zozeer om de beloning, maar eerder om mezelf te testen. Ik ben hier achter gekomen met wat hulp. Ik liep in zekere zin te strijden in mijn hoofd op de Havo, omdat ik wel wist dat ik er geen moeite mee had, maar er niet voor koos om er dan ook tijd in te steken. Hierdoor lukte het niet.
