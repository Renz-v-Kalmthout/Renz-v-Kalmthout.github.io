My thoughts on this Study:

I think this course suits me. I do need to rethread a lot of ground already covered, but that has re-ignited the flame for coding that I lost a few months ago. Coding thus far has been a really relaxing, but sometimes frustrating experience.

My thoughts on the hbo level:

The hbo so far is very relaxed and puts a lot of responsibility on the student itself. This can be a hurdle for me as I usually need a little push or reminder to start doing something. However the liberated feel of only having one goal, gives you a lot of freedom to go at your own pace.
