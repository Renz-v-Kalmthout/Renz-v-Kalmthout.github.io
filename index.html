My thoughts on this course:

I think this course suits me. I do need to rethread a lot of ground already covered,

My thoughts on the hbo level:

The hbo so far is very relaxed and puts a lot of responsibility on the student itself.
This can be a hurdle for me as I usually need a little push or reminder to start doing something.
However the liberated feel of only having one goal, gives you a lot of freedom to go at your own pace.
